About our company

Leon Finance and business centre is a christian microfinance and a non-Deposit taking institution regulated and supervised by (UMRA) Uganda Microfinance Regulatory Authority registered under tier4 from inception.

Leon Finance has grown rapidly to occupy almost all the districts in greater Masaka region by providing quality financial and non financial services to the active poor with its key concept of preaching the Gospel.
